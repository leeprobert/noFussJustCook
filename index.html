About us

We think cooking should be fun

This is why we have put together a range of day time courses that anyone can achieve no jargon and no skills needed. These will include Afternoon tea baking day, authentic curries to share and everyday family meals.

You will cook everything yourself so that you will have the confidence to make these dishes at home.

All ingredients and foil packaging will be provided for ease and no fuss.

Our venue is set in its own grounds in Tunbridge Wells and has free parking.

The Experience

The Experience

An amazing day out that you wont forget

Your day will start at 10am where we will welcome you with coffee.

We will show you step by step how to cook your chosen dishes, we should break for lunch at 1pm, we may sample some of your cooking but if this is not practical, lunch will be provided.

After lunch we will make your take home dish and aim to finish at around 3pm.

Our day will be relaxed, informal, informative and fun.
